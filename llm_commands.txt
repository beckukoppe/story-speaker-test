User->Story:
- #SPEAKWITH: <speaker_name> -> Respond with startprompt
- #CONTEXT <speaker_name>: <last_interaction> -> last interaction from speaker for story context

User->Speaker:
- #SAY: <content> -> say something to speaker-llm
- #SUMUP -> speaker llm should sum up the conversation for the story llm

Story-LLM:
- #STARTPROPMPT: <speaker_startprompt> -> startpropmt for speaker LLM
- #NOTHING: -> when there is nothing to say (#CONTEXT)

Speaker-LLM:
- #SPEAK: <content> -> text that gets generated to speech
- #SUM: <content> -> sum up of conversation
